About

Hi, I am Yitong.

I am a PhD candidate at Jilin University. My primary research interests include (i) federated learning and privacy-preserving machine learning, (ii) robust optimization for reliable AI systems, (iii) high-performance networking for distributed intelligence, (iv) bioinformatics and biomedical data mining. I am actively exploring the broader impacts of my research experience, particularly in secure, efficient, and trustworthy AI systems for biomedical applications and public health.
